# CogniPrint Reviewer Bundle — Reviewer Questions

Please answer only what you can assess. A short critical response is acceptable.

## Methodology

1. Are the mathematical objects and assumptions defined clearly enough for independent review?
2. Are the coordinate-stability and non-degeneracy assumptions appropriately bounded?
3. Are the perturbation/stability statements correctly framed as conditional?
4. Are Euclidean distance and cosine similarity suitable descriptive tools for this release stage?

## Empirical protocol

5. Is the public diagnostic layer sufficient for a descriptive release?
6. Are the benchmark provenance notes clear enough?
7. Are the reproduction instructions adequate for independent inspection?
8. What additional benchmark coverage would be needed before stronger claims could be considered?

## Claims and limitations

9. Does any part of the public package appear to overclaim?
10. Are the non-claims around authorship, AI origin, forensic identity, legal status, and universal classification sufficiently clear?
11. Are the limitations strong enough to prevent misuse?
12. What risks should be added to the public documentation?

## Review classification

Please indicate whether your response is:

- methodological review;
- informal comment;
- decline;
- acknowledgement only.

Only a substantive methodological assessment may be counted toward the external review counter.
