# CogniPrint Reviewer Bundle — Reproducibility

## Public entry points

- Repository: `https://github.com/TakoVHS/CogniPrint-open`
- Release: `https://github.com/TakoVHS/CogniPrint-open/releases/tag/v0.1.2`
- DOI: `https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20756421`

## Reproduction expectations

A reviewer should be able to inspect:

1. installation instructions in `README.md`;
2. citation metadata in `CITATION.cff`;
3. current status in `docs/current-state-summary.md`;
4. manuscript source in `paper/main.tex`;
5. public benchmark material in `datasets/`;
6. evidence artifacts in `evidence/`;
7. release and secret-scan tooling in `scripts/`;
8. CI workflow under `.github/workflows/`.

## Reproducibility questions

Please evaluate:

- whether the public instructions are sufficient;
- whether benchmark provenance is clear;
- whether the diagnostic layer can be independently rerun;
- whether private or administrative material appears excluded;
- whether the public release is adequate for methodological inspection.

## Boundary

Reproducibility of the release does not imply scientific validation. It only means the public artifacts are available and inspectable.
