# Data license and provenance

Repository-created evidence summaries, derived tables, benchmark metadata, manifests, and documentation-authored research artifacts are available under CC BY 4.0 unless a source-specific note is more restrictive.

Attribution:


Source-specific provenance and reuse notes in `datasets/`, `evidence/`, and `validation/` remain authoritative.

This license note does not change scientific readiness and does not turn CogniPrint into an authorship, source, AI-origin, legal, forensic, or universal classification system.
