# CogniPrint evidence dossier

CogniPrint computes an interpretable statistical feature vector for text and compares vectors using documented distance and similarity measures.

Public evidence includes bounded benchmark material, controlled perturbation outputs, descriptive validation artifacts, mathematical diagnostics, and manuscript source.

All current outputs are descriptive research measurements. They are not conclusions about identity, authorship, source, AI origin, legal status, or forensic provenance.
