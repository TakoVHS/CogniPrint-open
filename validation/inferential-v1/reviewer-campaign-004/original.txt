CogniPrint is gradually becoming a reproducible environment for empirical work on text-profile stability. In the current research programme, each campaign is designed around one baseline note and a set of controlled variants that preserve the same topic while changing one main dimension at a time. The purpose of this workflow is not to make definitive judgments about source or authorship. Instead, it is meant to record profile shifts, metric deltas, and campaign-level summaries that can later be reviewed by colleagues in a transparent way. This structure helps turn intuitive impressions about textual similarity into explicit empirical notes, tables, and provenance-aware research materials.
