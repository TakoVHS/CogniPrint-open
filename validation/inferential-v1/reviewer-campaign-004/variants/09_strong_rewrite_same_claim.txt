For empirical work on text-profile stability, CogniPrint is gradually becoming a reproducible environment. In the current research programme, around one baseline note is designed each campaign together with a set of controlled variants that preserve the same topic while changing one main dimension at a time. Not to make definitive judgments about source or authorship is the purpose of this workflow. Instead, by colleagues in a transparent way can later be reviewed the profile shifts, metric deltas, and campaign-level summaries that it records. This structure helps turn intuitive impressions about textual similarity into explicit empirical notes, tables, and provenance-aware research materials.
