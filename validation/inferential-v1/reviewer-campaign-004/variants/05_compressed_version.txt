CogniPrint is a reproducible environment for empirical work on text-profile stability. Each campaign uses one baseline note and controlled variants that keep the same topic while changing one main dimension at a time. The workflow is not meant to make definitive judgments about source or authorship. Instead, it records profile shifts, metric deltas, and campaign summaries for transparent review. This helps turn intuitive similarity impressions into explicit empirical notes and provenance-aware materials.
