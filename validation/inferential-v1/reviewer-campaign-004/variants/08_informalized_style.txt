CogniPrint is gradually turning into a repeatable setup for empirical work on text-profile stability. Right now, each campaign uses one baseline note and a set of controlled variants that keep the same topic but change one main thing at a time. The point of this workflow is not to make final calls about source or authorship. Instead, it records profile shifts, metric changes, and campaign summaries that colleagues can later review in a clear way. This helps turn vague impressions about textual similarity into explicit notes, tables, and provenance-aware research materials.
