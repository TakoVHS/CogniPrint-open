CogniPrint is gradually becoming a reproducible environment for empirical analysis of text-profile stability. In the current research programme, each campaign is organised around one baseline note and a group of controlled variants that preserve the same topic while modifying one principal dimension at a time. The purpose of this workflow is not to deliver definitive judgments about source or authorship. Instead, it is intended to record profile shifts, metric differences, and campaign-level summaries that can later be examined by colleagues in a transparent way. This structure helps convert intuitive impressions about textual similarity into explicit empirical notes, tables, and provenance-aware research materials.
