The CogniPrint project is moving toward a workflow in which empirical observations about text stability can be documented in a repeatable form. Rather than relying on a single note in isolation, the method places one baseline text next to several controlled rewrites that stay on the same topic while altering only selected dimensions. The intention is not to pronounce final conclusions about who wrote a text or where it came from. Instead, the system collects observable differences in profile structure, numerical metrics, and campaign-level summaries that can be examined later with colleagues. In practical terms, the workflow is meant to replace informal intuition with explicit empirical materials that are easier to inspect, compare, and reuse in manuscript preparation.
