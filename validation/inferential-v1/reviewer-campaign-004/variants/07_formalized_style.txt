CogniPrint is gradually being developed into a reproducible environment for empirical investigation of text-profile stability. Within the present research programme, each campaign is structured around a baseline note and a collection of controlled variants that preserve the same thematic content while varying one principal perturbation axis at a time. The purpose of this workflow is not to deliver definitive judgments concerning source or authorship. Rather, it is intended to record profile shifts, metric deltas, and campaign-level summaries that may subsequently be reviewed by colleagues in a transparent manner. In this way, intuitive impressions of textual similarity are converted into explicit empirical notes, tables, and provenance-aware research materials.
