# Cover Note

## Manuscript

**Cognitive Fingerprints: A Mathematical Framework for Statistical Profiling of Text**

## Author

Adriashkin Roman  
ORCID: https://orcid.org/0009-0009-6337-1806

## Summary

This manuscript introduces a conservative mathematical framework for representing text samples by compact finite-dimensional profiles, termed cognitive fingerprints. The paper defines the profile representation, introduces profile comparison in feature space, states conditional stability bounds under explicit assumptions, and records a reproducible empirical protocol for future work.

## Positioning

The manuscript is intended as a mathematical and computational research preprint. It does not claim definitive inference about authorship, source, or any other categorical label. Its goal is to provide a formal language for profile construction, profile comparison, and perturbation analysis.
